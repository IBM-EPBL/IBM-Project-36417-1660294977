Precautions During Rain

#1. Avoid farming.
#2. Equip yourself with an umbrella or a raincoat.
#3. Never touch electric wires in farm field.
#4. Take precautions against bugs.
#5. Clear a way for water flow towards outside farmland.
#6. Remove damaged Crops.
#7. Take care of yourself and animal livestocks.
#8. Eat and drink healthy.
